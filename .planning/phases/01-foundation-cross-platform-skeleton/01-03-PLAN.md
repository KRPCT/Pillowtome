<objective>
Lock the three day-1 architectural seams as minimal stubs and land the identity + change-log schema and the three key decision docs (Success Criterion 5). The seams — a format-agnostic `Publication` trait (EPUB-only impl, D-07), a composite self-healing `Locator` (D-08), and the opaque `BookSource` storage-handle (D-05) — plus a UUID + content-hash + per-device append-only change-log SQLite schema (D-09) — exist NOW, unsynced, so annotations (P5) and sync (P7) are additive rather than a Lithium-style late refactor.

Purpose: These abstractions must be concrete before any feature binds to them. Introducing them while only EPUB exists is the whole anti-refactor game. The schema ships versioned from v1 so later phases migrate forward, never rewrite.
Output: Filled `publication`, `locator`, `source` core stubs; a `tauri-plugin-sql` v1 migration with a migration smoke test; and DEC-001/002/003 decision records.
</objective>
